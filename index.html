David J Reiss, PhD

For a little (not much) more about me, visit my UW profile.

I am currently a Data Management Research Scientist for the Large Synoptic Survey Telescope, at the University of Washington dept. of Astronomy. I research algorithms and develop software for image subtraction and transient detection. I am interested in detection of all kinds of transients, particularly supernovae.

  • PhD Thesis: "The Rate of Supernovae in the Nearby and Distant Universe" (1999)
In my most recent previous life, I was a Senior Computational Systems Biology Research Scientist at the Institute for Systems Biology.
